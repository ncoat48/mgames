How to Play

Guide Pac-Man through the maze to eat all the dots while avoiding the ghosts!

  • PC: Use arrow keys to move
  • Mobile: Use the directional buttons
  • Eat power pellets to temporarily turn the tables on the ghosts
  • Clear all dots to advance to the next level
  • Adjust game speed using the speed controls

Good luck and have fun!
